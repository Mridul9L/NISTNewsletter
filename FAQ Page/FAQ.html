Frequently Asked Questions

The purpose of the newsletter project is to showcase the collaborative work of Grade 9 students from NIST International School across six different teams.

The teams were formed based on common interests and skills, ensuring a diverse range of perspectives and talents in each group.

The newsletter covers a wide range of topics, including but not limited to: school events, student achievements, community initiatives, academic updates, and creative endeavors.

The teams collaborated through regular meetings, brainstorming sessions, sharing of ideas and resources, and dividing tasks based on individual strengths and interests.
